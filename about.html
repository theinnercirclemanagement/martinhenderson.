Story

Born in Auckland, New Zealand, Martin Henderson began acting on the soap opera Shortland Street before moving to Australia and later the United States.

He is known for his roles as Dr. Nathan Riggs in Grey’s Anatomy, Sean McNamara in Nashville, and Jack Sheridan in Virgin River — characters shaped by loss, love, and second chances.

Off-screen, he values privacy, music, and the quiet moments between the scenes.

Approach

Martin approaches each role with emotional honesty, avoiding grand gestures in favor of stillness, presence, and truth.

“I’m not interested in heroes,” he once said. “I’m interested in people trying to do better.”
